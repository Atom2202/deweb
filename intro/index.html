Intro

World Map

The map is a 1:1000 scale earth replica. Cobblestone walls represent country borders. Ground cobblestone represents population density. Deposits of minerals are based on real data.

View the live world map at datearth.com/map.

Towns

Towns are safe from raiding and looting unless the towns nation is at war. It costs $1500 to start a town and each chunk claim for the town costs $250.

Type /help towns in-game for commands and more information.

Nations

Nations are a collection of towns with a town as capital. It cost $10000 to form a new nation.

Type /help nations in-game for commands and more information.

Wars

Nations can declare war against other nations to overtake their towns.

Type /help wars in-game for commands and more information.

Economy

Jobs

Money can be earned by doings jobs and completing daily quests. Type /help jobs in-game for commands and more information.

Shops

Players can create chest shops to trade with other players. Join a town and a nation to teleport to trading hubs. Type /help shops in-game for commands and more information.

Armoury

Weapons

Shift to aim, left click to shoot and right click to reload.

Purchase weapons with /weapons in-game.

Vehicles

Purchase vehicles with /vehicles command.
